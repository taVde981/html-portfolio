My Top 3 Horror Movie Recommendations

The Wailing

If someone asks me to recommend a horror movie to watch, this movie is always at the tip of my tongue. Movie plot is wild. The struggle to uncover the mystery of who is spreading "the plague" in Korea takes the viewer on a terrifying journy of ups and downs.

The Medium

This movie is the definition of bone chilling horror. The actreass has done a tremendous job of acting being possessed. The other part of the scare comes from the religious connotations made in the movie. It's definitely worth watching.

Bramayugam

Bramayugam is a malayalam horror film which grips the viewers attention from the beginning. Although the film is shot in the year 2024, the movie has no colors, it is black and white movie and this adds to the unhinged creepiness of the movie as our eyes are habitual to see colors. Worth watching!
